🎨 ZONGHENG NovelEngine: Industrialized Production Protocol for Long-Form Narrative AI

Breaking the “intelligence degradation” curse of long-form storytelling, pushing online fiction creation from “empiricism” to an engineering revolution based on “parametrization.”

🧠 During AI writing, you may have encountered these problems:
● Too few prompts → poor writing; too many prompts → loss of creativity  
Why not break AI writing into an engineering pipeline with division of labor?

● Too much material → AI misses settings, plot points, hooks  
Why not constrain different AI behaviors with different rules? Rule cages + evolving characters + isolated AI

● Characters seem to speak, but lack real goals and emotions  
Why not build a virtual world where the protagonist explores goals, side characters force choices, and AI information is isolated?

● Weak payoff, insufficient climax, conflicts feel procedural  
Why treat “highlight moments” as summaries rather than goals? Decompose them with engineering methods, plan business goals and emotional peaks first, then amplify through templates, environments, and ensemble casts.

● Unstable analysis, assets and foreshadowing get lost  
Why not separate goals and deduction? Goals recover hooks, deduction ensures realism.

● Automation exists but still requires manual rework  
Why not define assets and hooks first + objective auditing, eliminating ambiguity in summaries?

● Content is generated, but always slightly off target  
Why not introduce a “finishing touch” role, letting a chief editor refine and feed back audit suggestions?

● Tools get more complex, writing becomes harder  
Why not let the system evolve itself, becoming smarter with use?

Imagine if all these problems were solved — this would no longer be just a content generator, but a virtual world simulator.

📑 Vision: Solving the “Long-Form Termination” Problem in AI Creation

Most current AI writing tools remain at “expansion” or “single-chapter generation.” As length increases, issues like setting pollution, logical collapse, character distortion (OOC), and broken foreshadowing inevitably arise.

NovelEngine is not a simple prompt collection, but a novel production protocol based on Multi-Agent Relay and a Dynamic Constraint System.  
We aim to transform uncontrollable inspiration into a predictable, reversible, and auditable industrial pipeline.

🚀 Core Advantages: Why we can produce long-form works without “intelligence degradation”

1. Five-Layer Character Model: Consistency Engine  
A dynamic system ensuring characters remain themselves even as they evolve, from philosophical origin (Layer 0) to conflict feedback (Layer 4).

2. Multi-Agent Non-Omniscient Game  
Each character is played by an independent LLM instance with minimal information. The protagonist’s decisions are forced outcomes of interactions—not author-imposed.

3. World Rule Locks & Scene Executor  
The world is treated as an operating system. Rules actively constrain events (e.g., magic consumes mental energy—no cheating when depleted).

4. Dual Helix of Emotion and Plot  
Parameterized control aligns narrative progression with reader emotions.

5. Foreshadowing Ledger  
Tracks lifecycle of setups and activates them at optimal moments.

6. Quantitative Iteration  
Multi-dimensional iteration across models, market, and system.

🛠️ Six-Layer Architecture: Full Production Pipeline

World Model: Defines physical rules and knowledge graph  
Plot Planning: Locks business hooks and emotional arcs  
Deduction Outline: Multi-agent simulation for conflict-driven beats  
Text Generation: Multi-role collaboration under constraints  
Audit & Optimization: Objective + subjective dual-track review  
Knowledge Accumulation: Stores facts and updates state for next chapter

💎 Data & Control Logic

Single Source of Truth (PgSQL): All facts and states  
Semantic Layer (Vector DB): Worldbuilding, style, trope library  
Control Mode: Parameter input → simulation → differential audit
